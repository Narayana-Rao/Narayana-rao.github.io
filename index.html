About

Postdoctoral Fellow

  • Affiliation/s: UMass Amherst and Caltech
  • Email: narayanarao.bhogapurapu@gmail.com

My research focuses on advancing Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) techniques for soil moisture estimation, infrastructure monitoring, ground deformation mapping, and vegetation analysis. I have developed novel methodologies to estimate soil moisture over croplands using dual-pol GRD SAR, full, and compact polarimetric SAR data, enabling large-scale, high-resolution monitoring. Additionally, I have proposed vegetation descriptors for dual-pol GRD SAR that support both soil moisture estimation and crop biophysical parameter retrieval. These techniques are implemented on AWS, Google Earth Engine, and Google Colab, with potential applications in operational crop monitoring platforms. Currently, I‚Äôm working on forest canopy height and biomass estimation using InSAR and LiDAR. My career goal is to secure a research position in remote sensing to contribute to scientific progress and national development.
